🍪 Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help remember your preferences and improve your experience.

How We Use Cookies

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Service functionality, security Session
Analytics Understanding site usage 12 months
Marketing Personalized content 6 months

Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Cookies
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Third-Party Cookies

We use services that may set cookies:

  • Stripe - Payment processing cookies
  • Analytics tools - Usage analytics
