📋 Content Policy

1. Acceptable Use

BotForge is designed for legitimate business purposes. You may use our service to:

  • Create customer support chatbots
  • Build internal business assistants
  • Automate FAQ responses
  • Engage with website visitors
  • Process sales inquiries

2. Prohibited Content

You may NOT use BotForge to create chatbots that:

  • Generate illegal content or advice
  • Promote violence, hate speech, or discrimination
  • Harass, bully, or threaten others
  • Distribute malware or phishing attempts
  • Engage in fraud or scams
  • Violate intellectual property rights
  • Generate sexually explicit content
  • Provide medical, legal, or financial advice (without proper disclaimers)
  • Impersonate real persons or organizations

3. AI-Generated Content

⚠️ Important: AI chatbots may occasionally produce inaccurate, inappropriate, or unexpected outputs. You are responsible for:
  • Reviewing and moderating your chatbot's responses
  • Setting appropriate content filters
  • Adding disclaimers where necessary
  • Monitoring conversations for abuse

4. DMCA & Copyright

We respect intellectual property rights. If you believe your content has been infringed:

  1. Identify the specific content and its location
  2. Provide your contact information
  3. State you have rights to the content or represent the rights holder
  4. Send DMCA notice to: legal@botforge.ai

5. Data You Provide

You are responsible for ensuring you have rights to:

  • Documents uploaded for chatbot training
  • Business information used in chatbot configuration
  • Customer data processed through your chatbots

6. Enforcement

We may, at our sole discretion:

  • Remove or disable access to chatbots violating this policy
  • Suspend or terminate accounts
  • Report illegal content to authorities

7. Reporting Violations

Report content policy violations to: legal@botforge.ai
