🚀 Getting Started

Your bot will be live in 4 simple steps

1

Complete Your Profile

Log into your dashboard and fill in your business details. Tell us about your products, services, and typical customer questions.

2

Upload Knowledge Base

Add documents, FAQs, or paste text for your bot to learn from. Supported formats: PDF, TXT, DOCX, or just paste text directly.

💡 Pro Tip

The more relevant content you add, the better your bot will answer questions!

3

Customize Appearance

Make your bot match your brand:
• Choose colors that match your website
• Upload your logo or avatar
• Set a friendly greeting message

4

Add to Your Website

Copy the embed code from your dashboard and paste it just before the closing </body> tag on your website.

📋 Embed Code

<script src="https://botforge.ai/widget.js"></script>

✓

You're Live! 🎉

That's it! Your bot is now live and ready to help customers. We'll notify you when people start chatting.
